About us

The Company

Torrent Software is a start-up company co-founded in 2012 by renowned peer-to-peer technology developer Pablo Soto, along with some of the finest angel investors and technology designers in Madrid, Spain. Built around a culture that encourages making dreams come true through smart thinking, hard work, and Free Culture, Torrent Software is devoted to fulfill the information and entertainment needs for millions of users worldwide.
